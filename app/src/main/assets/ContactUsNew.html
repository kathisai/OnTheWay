Grievance Redressal Procedure:

The below is the procedure for any grievance redressal:

  • 1. Passenger first writes to Customer Relations on custrelations@spicejet.com and SpiceJet’s Customer Relations assigns a unique Customer Relation Number to the Customer when he registers his/her Grievance at SpiceJet Customer Care.
  • 2. If you are not satisfied with the response of SpiceJet’s Customer Relations, then you may contact the Nodal Officer quoting your unique Customer Relation Number at nodalofficer@spicejet.com
  • 3. If you are still not satisfied and wish to appeal, you may appeal with our Appellate Authority at appellateauthority@spicejet.com quoting your unique Customer Relation Number.
